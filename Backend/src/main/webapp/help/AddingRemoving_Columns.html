Adding/Removing Columns

In many tabular reports (reports that are shown as tables, as opposed to graphical and form reports), you can add columns to or remove columns from the report by clicking  (Add/Remove Fields button) on the top right of the report. In the pop up menu that opens, select/clear the required Columns in order to add/remove columns to the report. Click  (Close button) to close the pop up menu.

Note: The newly added columns are always added to the end of the list. You can later rearrange the columns order.
